# SignSpeakers  
Breaking Communication Barriers with Technology

SignSpeakers is a real-time sign language recognition system that converts sign language gestures into text and speech, empowering the deaf and hard of hearing community to communicate seamlessly with everyone.

---

## The Global Challenge

• 1.5B+ people globally affected by hearing loss  
• 430M people with disabling hearing loss  
• 63M people in India with auditory impairment  

SignSpeakers aims to reduce this communication gap using AI and computer vision.

---

## Why SignSpeakers Matters

### Bridging Communication Gaps  
Enables seamless interaction between sign language users and non-signers without requiring a human interpreter.

### Enhancing Social Inclusion  
Helps individuals participate fully in education, social life, and public interactions.

### Improving Access to Services  
Ensures effective communication in healthcare, legal, and emergency situations.

### Educational Opportunities  
Supports students with hearing impairments in learning environments.

### Employment Access  
Reduces workplace communication barriers, opening up more job opportunities.

### Economic Impact  
Addressing hearing loss could save the global economy 980 billion dollars annually.

---

• Real-time sign language detection
